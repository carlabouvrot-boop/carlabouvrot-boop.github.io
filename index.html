Projects

Connected Greenhouse (IoT)

Arduino C/C++ Sensors Electronics IoT Prototyping

I was responsible for the electronic architecture and Arduino programming of a compact automated greenhouse designed for autonomous irrigation and ventilation control.

Electronics

  • Designed the entire electronic system.
  • Integrated sensors: temperature, humidity and soil moisture.
  • Controlled a 12 V ventilation fan via relays.
  • Integrated a solenoid valve for drip irrigation.
  • Managed power distribution (5V/12V) and wiring reliability.
  • Helped design and organize the PCB for cleaner cable routing.

Arduino Programming

  • Developed all automation logic in C/C++.
  • Fan activation based on temperature thresholds.
  • Irrigation triggered by soil moisture readings.
  • Modular, robust code for easier debugging and scalability.
  • Performed multiple integration tests and sensor calibration.

Results

  • Fully functioning automated greenhouse.
  • Reliable 24/7 operation of sensors and actuators.
  • Compact and eco-friendly structure (wood + plexiglass).
  • Validated as part of the ICAM project milestones.

A complete end-to-end IoT system combining sensors, embedded programming and hardware integration — delivered as part of my engineering program.

Academic Travel (Erasmus)

Technion – Israel Institute of Technology, Haifa — 2022-2023

Spending a semester at Technion in Haifa was one of the most formative experiences of my studies. I joined the Faculty of Mechanical Engineering, where I strengthened my technical skills in mechanical design, thermodynamics, and materials science through hands-on projects and laboratory work. Living in a multicultural city — where Jewish, Arab, and international communities coexist — taught me to adapt quickly, communicate across cultures, and appreciate diverse perspectives. Beyond academics, daily life in Israel was a lesson in independence: navigating administrative challenges, discovering unique traditions like Shabbat, and experiencing a completely new rhythm of life. This exchange deeply enhanced my open-mindedness, resilience, and ability to thrive in international and multidisciplinary environments.

UIT The Arctic University of Norway, Narvik — 2024

Immersed in a Nordic approach to sustainable engineering that combines technical precision with environmental responsibility. Courses and projects focused on renewable energy, life-cycle analysis, Lean methods, and Industry 4.0, fostering a mindset where efficiency and sustainability go hand in hand. Surrounded by the Arctic landscapes of Northern Norway, I learned how nature and engineering coexist — how every design choice must respect resources, endure harsh climates, and remain simple yet durable. This experience deepened my commitment to eco-design and pragmatic innovation, where long-term impact matters more than short-term performance.
